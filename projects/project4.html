Customer Churn Prediction

Customer Churn Prediction is an end-to-end machine learning application that identifies telecom customers at risk of churning — giving companies the opportunity to intervene before losing them.

The project includes a full ML pipeline built with Pandas for preprocessing, scikit-learn for model training across Logistic Regression, Random Forest, and XGBoost, and Joblib for model serialization. An interactive Streamlit frontend lets users input customer data and receive an instant churn probability score, along with feature importance visualizations explaining the key drivers of churn.
