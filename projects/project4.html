Customer Churn Prediction

Streamlit scikit-learn Logistic Regression GridSearchCV Joblib

Customer Churn Prediction uses machine learning to flag telecom customers likely to churn, based on demographic and service-usage data, and serves predictions through a live Streamlit web app.

Dataset

  • Source: Telecom Customer Churn (Kaggle). Target: Churn (Yes = 1, No = 0).
  • Features: Age, Gender, Tenure, MonthlyCharges.

What I built

  • Ran EDA and preprocessing, scaling inputs with a StandardScaler serialized alongside the model.
  • Trained and compared five classifiers — Logistic Regression, Random Forest, SVM, KNN, and Decision Tree — evaluating on accuracy, precision, recall, and F1.
  • Tuned each with GridSearchCV (C, penalty, class_weight) and selected Logistic Regression as the best balance of validation performance, generalization, and interpretability.
  • Serialized the model and scaler with Joblib and shipped a Streamlit app that returns an instant churn prediction from entered customer details.
