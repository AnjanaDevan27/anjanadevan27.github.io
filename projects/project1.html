SellerCentral Chatbot

SellerCentral Chatbot is an AI-powered virtual assistant that enables Amazon sellers to ask natural language questions about their product metrics, inventory, and overall store performance.

The solution integrates LangChain for orchestrating LLM capabilities, FastAPI for backend services, and LLaMA as the core model. It is containerized using Docker, scheduled through Airflow, and deployed to production on Google Cloud Platform (GCP).
