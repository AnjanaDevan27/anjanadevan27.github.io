The Beauty of Safety

The Beauty of Safety is a machine learning project that classifies cosmetic chemicals by toxicity level using publicly available ingredient datasets — helping consumers and researchers understand what's really inside their beauty products.

The pipeline was built with Pandas for data cleaning and feature engineering, scikit-learn for training and comparing classification models including Random Forest, SVM, and LightGBM, and Matplotlib for visualizing toxicity distributions across product categories. Class imbalance was addressed using SMOTE oversampling and cost-sensitive learning.
