DineTrack

DineTrack is a full-stack restaurant visit analytics platform that tracks dining habits, ratings, and spending patterns — turning raw visit data into actionable business intelligence insights.

The backend is powered by MySQL hosted on GCP Cloud SQL, with a normalized relational schema for restaurants, visits, and users. Data is connected to R via the DBI package, and interactive BI dashboards are built using ggplot2 and Shiny for time-series reporting and visit frequency analysis.
